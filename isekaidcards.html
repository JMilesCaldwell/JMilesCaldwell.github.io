LEVEL 1 - Choose Your Path

PATH OF THE WARRIOR

Level 1 Choice

+10 HP
Proficiencies
Martial weapons, shields, heavy armour
Fighting Style
Choose one:
• Defense: +1 AC while wearing armour
• Great Weapon Fighting: Reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice (melee, two-handed weapons)
Second Wind
Bonus action: Recover 1d10 + your level HP. Once per short rest.

PATH OF THE MAGE

Level 1 Choice

+6 HP
Spellcasting (INT)
Cantrips: Choose 3
• Fire Bolt, Ray of Frost, Mage Hand

Level 1 Spells: 4 slots
• Magic Missile, Shield, Burning Hands
• Thunderwave, Sleep, Mage Armor
Arcane Recovery
Once per day on a short rest, recover spell slots totaling half your level (rounded up).

PATH OF THE ROGUE

Level 1 Choice

+8 HP
Proficiencies
Light armour, thieves' tools
Sneak Attack
+2d6 damage once per turn when you have advantage on the attack roll, or when an ally is within 5 feet of your target.
Cunning Action
Use a bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide.

LEVEL 2 - Warrior Specializations

PATH OF THE KNIGHT

Level 2 - Warrior Path

+10 HP, +1 AC
Protection
When a creature you can see attacks a target other than you that is within 5 feet of you, use your reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll.
Action Surge
Take one additional action on your turn. Once per short rest.

PATH OF THE BERSERKER

Level 2 - Warrior Path

+12 HP
Rage
Bonus action: Gain +2 damage on melee attacks, resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage, and advantage on STR checks and saves. Lasts 1 minute. 2 uses per day.
Reckless Attack
When you make your first attack on your turn, gain advantage on all melee weapon attacks this turn. Attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn.

LEVEL 2 - Mage Specializations

PATH OF THE WIZARD

Level 2 - Mage Path

+6 HP
Enhanced Spellcasting
Level 1 Slots: 6 total
Level 2 Slots: 3

New Spells:
• Scorching Ray, Misty Step
• Hold Person, Shatter
Sculpt Spells
When you cast an evocation spell, choose a number of creatures equal to 1 + the spell's level. They automatically succeed on their saves and take no damage.

PATH OF THE NECROMANCER

Level 2 - Mage Path

+6 HP
Enhanced Spellcasting
Level 1 Slots: 6 total
Level 2 Slots: 3

New Spells:
• Ray of Enfeeblement, Blindness/Deafness
• Animate Dead (creates 1 skeleton or zombie)
Grim Harvest
When you kill a creature with a spell, regain HP equal to twice the spell's level (three times for necromancy spells).

LEVEL 2 - Rogue Specializations

PATH OF THE ASSASSIN

Level 2 - Rogue Path

+8 HP
Sneak Attack
Increases to 3d6 damage.
Assassinate
You have advantage on initiative rolls. Any hit you score on a surprised creature is a critical hit.
Poisoner
Proficiency with poisoner's kit. You can apply poison to a weapon as a bonus action.

PATH OF THE THIEF

Level 2 - Rogue Path

+8 HP
Sneak Attack
Increases to 3d6 damage.
Fast Hands
Use Cunning Action to make a Sleight of Hand check, use thieves' tools to disarm traps or pick locks, or take the Use an Object action.
Second-Story Work
Climbing no longer costs extra movement. Your running jump distance increases by your DEX modifier.
