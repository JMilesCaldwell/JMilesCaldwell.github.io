# Lifestyle — Claude Context

## What This Is
A creative playground for **satirical pieces and fun HTML projects**. Loosely inspired by [lileks.com](https://lileks.com/) — the James Lileks archive of retro pop-culture artifacts, regrettable food, regrettable architecture, midcentury advertising, and dryly affectionate commentary on all of it.

The folder name "lifestyle" is legacy — think of this as the "bits and bobs" drawer. Anything satirical, loving-pastiche, nostalgia-tinged, or just weird-for-weird's-sake belongs here. Not tied to a single language, topic, or style.

## Spiritual Predecessors
When unsure about tone or approach, lileks.com is the north star:
- **Gallery of Regrettable Food** — lovingly catalogued, gently mocked
- **Institute of Official Cheer** — sincere-looking sendups
- **Motel postcards, matchbook covers, Art Frahm cheesecake ads** — found ephemera with commentary
- Dry, affectionate, not mean-spirited. The joke is the curation, not the contempt.

## Working Principles
- **Each piece is its own self-contained HTML file.** Inline everything. Don't try to build shared infrastructure — the variety is the point.
- **Intentional bad taste is still good taste.** Do not "fix" Comic Sans, blink animations, marquees, garish colors, or other chosen crimes against web design. If something looks like a 1997 GeoCities fever dream, it's probably correct.
- **Match the vibe of the piece**, not the vibe of the folder. Each satire sets its own rules.
- **Commit per piece.** One new satirical page = one focused commit.

## Language
Content so far is in Dutch (NL), but the folder isn't Dutch-only. Future pieces can be in any language the joke requires. Keep the Dutch pages in Dutch.
