Concept:
A shitty looking space-invaders style game, that uses .png vectorized images of cats as the 'attackers', and a .png vectorized image of kibble as projectiles. The player's 'ship' is a vectorized food bowl.
Certain vectorized cats, named 'chonkers' are level bosses. These take a lot of kibble to pacify. If a cat is fed it flies off to the side.
