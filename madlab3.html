DM Cheat Sheet - Official 5e Content

Conditions & Status Effects

These conditions modify a creature’s capabilities in various ways (spells, monster abilities, etc.).

  • Blinded: Cannot see, automatically fails sight-based checks, attackers have advantage.
  • Charmed: Cannot attack the charmer; the charmer has advantage on social checks.
  • Deafened: Cannot hear, automatically fails hearing-based checks.
  • Frightened: Disadvantage on checks/attacks while source of fear is in sight.
  • Grappled: Speed 0; ends if grappler is incapacitated or forced away.
  • Incapacitated: Cannot take actions or reactions.
  • Invisible: Cannot be seen without special senses; attackers have disadvantage.
  • Paralyzed: Incapacitated, can’t move or speak, fails Str/Dex saves; hits within 5 ft. are crits.
  • Petrified: Transformed into a solid substance; incapacitated.
  • Poisoned: Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.
  • Prone: Movement cost doubles to stand, disadvantage on attacks unless attacker is within 5 ft.
  • Restrained: Speed 0; disadvantage on attack rolls and Dex saves.
  • Stunned: Incapacitated, can’t move, fails Str/Dex saves.
  • Unconscious: Incapacitated, drops held items, prone, fails Str/Dex saves, hits within 5 ft. are crits.
  • Exhaustion: 6 levels; from disadvantage on checks up to death at level 6.

Travel & Movement
  • Overland Pace:
    • Fast: 4 mph (–5 Passive Perception)
    • Normal: 3 mph
    • Slow: 2 mph (can use Stealth)
  • Forced March: After 8 hours, CON save (DC 10 + 1 per hour) or gain exhaustion.
  • Visibility & Encounter Distance: Adjust for weather, terrain, and time of day.
Combat Mechanics & Actions

Common actions include Attack, Cast a Spell, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Grapple, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Shove, Use Object.

Cover:

  • Half Cover: +2 to AC / Dex saves
  • Three-Quarters Cover: +5 to AC / Dex saves
  • Total Cover: Cannot be targeted

Encounter Building & XP Thresholds

Use XP thresholds for Easy/Medium/Hard/Deadly based on party level. Adjust for multiple foes (DMG multipliers).

Damage & Healing
  • Falling: 1d6 per 10 ft. (max 20d6)
  • Suffocating: CON mod in minutes, then risk dropping to 0 hp
  • Resistance/Immunity/Vulnerability: Halve/Negate/Double certain damage types
  • Massive Damage: Could cause instant death if high enough
  • Death Saves: 3 fails = death, 3 successes = stable (1 = 2 fails, 20 = 1 hp immediately)

Environmental Hazards: Improvise DCs for hazards (icy wall DC 15, sandstorm DC 10 CON/hour), etc.

Social Interactions

NPC Attitudes:

  • Friendly: Likely to help or cooperate.
  • Indifferent: Neutral; can be swayed by roleplay or checks.
  • Hostile: Opposes or undermines the party.

Use Persuasion/Deception/Intimidation/Insight to influence or discern motives. Adjust DCs by context.
