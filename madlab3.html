Conditions & Status Effects

These conditions modify a creature’s capabilities in various ways (spells, monster abilities, etc.).

  • Blinded: Cannot see, automatically fails sight-based checks, attackers have advantage.
  • Charmed: Cannot attack the charmer; the charmer gains advantage on related social checks.
  • Deafened: Cannot hear, automatically fails hearing-based checks.
  • Frightened: Disadvantage on ability checks/attack rolls while the source of fear is in sight.
  • Grappled: Speed becomes 0; ends if the grappler is incapacitated or moved.
  • Incapacitated: Cannot take actions or reactions.
  • Invisible: Cannot be seen without special senses; attackers have disadvantage.
  • Paralyzed: Incapacitated, cannot move or speak, automatic failure on Str/Dex saves; hits within 5 ft. are critical.
  • Petrified: Transformed into a solid substance; incapacitated.
  • Poisoned: Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.
  • Prone: Movement cost doubles, disadvantage on attack rolls unless attacker is within 5 ft.
  • Restrained: Speed becomes 0; disadvantage on attacks and Dex saves.
  • Stunned: Incapacitated, can only speak falteringly, fails Str/Dex saves.
  • Unconscious: Incapacitated, drops held items, prone, automatic failures on Str/Dex saves.
  • Exhaustion: Levels 1–6, with increasingly severe effects culminating in death at level 6.

Travel & Movement
  • Overland Pace:
    • Fast: 4 miles/hour (–5 Passive Perception)
    • Normal: 3 miles/hour (no penalty)
    • Slow: 2 miles/hour (allows Stealth)
  • Forced March: Beyond 8 hours, a CON save is required (DC 10 + 1 per extra hour) or suffer exhaustion.
  • Visibility & Encounter Distance: Adjust based on weather, terrain, and time.
Combat Mechanics & Actions

Common actions include:

  • Attack, Cast a Spell, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Grapple, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Shove, Use an Object

Cover Rules:

  • Half Cover: +2 bonus to AC and Dex saves
  • Three-Quarters Cover: +5 bonus to AC and Dex saves
  • Total Cover: Cannot be targeted

Encounter Building & XP Thresholds

Use XP thresholds (Easy, Medium, Hard, Deadly) based on party level and size. Consult the DMG for full tables and adjust for multiple foes using multipliers.

Damage & Healing
  • Falling Damage: 1d6 per 10 ft. (max 20d6)
  • Suffocating: Can hold breath for CON mod (minimum 30 seconds), then risk dropping to 0 hp
  • Resistances/Immunities/Vulnerabilities: Adjust damage accordingly
  • Massive Damage: Can cause instant death if severe enough
  • Death Saving Throws: 3 successes stabilize; 3 failures result in death (a 1 counts as two failures; a 20 restores 1 hp immediately)

Lighting Conditions:

  • Bright Light: Normal vision
  • Dim Light: Disadvantage on sight-based Perception checks
  • Darkness: Creatures without darkvision are effectively blinded
Environmental Hazards: Improvise DCs (e.g. icy wall: DC 15; sandstorm: CON save DC 10/hour)

Social Interactions

NPC attitudes can be categorized as:

  • Friendly: Willing to help
  • Indifferent: Neutral unless persuaded
  • Hostile: Likely to oppose the party

Use Persuasion, Deception, Intimidation, or Insight checks to influence NPCs. Adjust DCs based on context and roleplay.
