Backstory
Vigge was an entertainer — a strongman making a living as part of a travelling circus. One particularly rainy day, he was helping to put up the circus tent when he was struck by lightning. When he woke up, he found that the storm had awakened something ancient and wild within him: a sorcerous connection to wind and thunder. He also felt a divine calling stir in his heart, as if the heavens themselves had chosen him.
