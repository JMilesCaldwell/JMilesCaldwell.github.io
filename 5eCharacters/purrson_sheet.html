Backstory
Tobias Purrson is the last survivor of his tribe, a Tabaxi who has made his living as a bounty hunter in the wild lands. He roams the forests and underdark, tracking dangerous prey—humanoid and monster alike—guided by an instinct honed through years of survival. What he lacks in social grace he makes up for in patience, precision, and a willingness to get the job done. He has little use for the trappings of civilization and less use for those who hide behind wealth or pretense.
