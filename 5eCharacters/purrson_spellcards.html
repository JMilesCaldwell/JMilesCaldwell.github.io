Protection from Evil and Good
1st-level · Abjuration Concentration Monster Slayer
Casting Time1 action
RangeTouch
ComponentsV, S, M (holy water or powdered silver and iron, consumed)
DurationConc., up to 10 min
Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead. Those creature types have disadvantage on attack rolls against the target. The target also can't be charmed, frightened, or possessed by them. If already under such an effect, the target has advantage on a new saving throw to end it.

Ensnaring Strike
1st-level · Conjuration Concentration
Casting Time1 bonus action
RangeSelf
ComponentsV
DurationConc., up to 1 min
Restrained · 1d6 piercing/turn (STR save DC 13)
The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack before this spell ends, thorny vines appear and the target must succeed on a STR save or be restrained. While restrained, it takes 1d6 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature restrained by the vines or one that can touch it can use an action to make a STR check against your spell save DC to free it.

Casting Time1 action
RangeSelf
ComponentsV, S
Duration10 minutes
You gain the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with beasts for the duration. Zone of Truth
2nd-level · Enchantment Monster Slayer CHA Save
Casting Time1 action
Range60 ft
ComponentsV, S
Duration10 minutes
15-ft radius · CHA save DC 13
You create a magical zone that guards against deception in a 15-foot-radius sphere. A creature that enters the zone for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must make a CHA saving throw. On a failed save, a creature can't speak a deliberate lie while in the radius. You know whether each creature succeeds or fails on its saving throw. An affected creature is aware of the spell and can thus avoid answering or be evasive. No concentration required.
Pass Without Trace
2nd-level · Abjuration Concentration
Casting Time1 action
RangeSelf
ComponentsV, S, M (ashes from burned mistletoe and a sprig of spruce)
DurationConc., up to 1 hour
+10 bonus to Stealth (party-wide)
A veil of shadows and silence radiates from you, masking you and your companions from detection. For the duration, each creature you choose within 30 feet of you (including you) has a +10 bonus to Dexterity (Stealth) checks and can't be tracked except by magical means. A creature that receives this bonus leaves behind no tracks or other traces of its passage. With Purrson's Stealth of +7, this means a total of +17.
